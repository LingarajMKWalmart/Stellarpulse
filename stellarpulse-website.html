About Stellarpulse

Stellarpulse is a non-profit collective dedicated to curating transcendental electronic music experiences across Switzerland. We believe music is a universal language that connects souls across the cosmos.

Since 2020, we've been pushing the boundaries of what an electronic music event can be - combining cutting-edge sound, immersive visuals, and sustainable practices to create unforgettable journeys through space and sound.

Our mission extends beyond entertainment. We support emerging artists, promote mental health awareness, and contribute to environmental causes through every event we organize.
